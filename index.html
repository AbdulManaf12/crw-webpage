About

This competition aims to promote and develop 21st-century case studies pertaining to Pakistani businesses and industries. There has been an increased need for a realistic preview of local, Pakistani-based case studies in HRM, Marketing, and Entrepreneurship. The main objective of this competition is the development and promotion of real-life and current case studies and dilemmas faced by business managers in the 21st century.

Business situations are dynamic and rapidly changing, hence requiring business practitioners with updated information and management practices that the world demands. The focus of this competition will be on managerial issues pertaining to Entrepreneurship, HRM, and Marketing in the Pakistani context. The proposed outcome of this competition is quality teaching case studies in the said business domains. The case studies will be used by industry and academic practitioners to understand, develop, apply, and create solutions to modern-day problems in these fields.

The case studies will focus on creating value for entrepreneurs, HRM practitioners, and marketers by applying strategies, creating processes, and developing solutions. After practicing the cases from this book, students, academicians, and researchers will be able to make informed decisions. This book will consist of short and long case studies with teaching notes across various industries and regions of Pakistan, providing the readers with valuable insights.
