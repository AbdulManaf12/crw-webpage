About the Competition

This competition aims to promote and develop 21st-century case studies in Pakistani businesses across HRM, Marketing, and Entrepreneurship. It offers students, academia, and professionals a chance to address real-world challenges.

Business situations are dynamic and rapidly changing, requiring business practitioners to stay updated with information and management practices. The focus of this competition is on managerial issues related to Entrepreneurship, HRM, and Marketing within a Pakistani context.
